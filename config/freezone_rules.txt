1. Active Visa/ Visa Issued:

17. Number of Company having Employees 
   - Primary Table: SF_Visa_LK_LOAD
   - Category Column: No_of_Employers_having_Employees (this means how many Companies are having 5,6,7,...100...10000 Employees)
   - SQL Query: SELECT CASE 
                WHEN EmpCount >= 1  AND EmpCount < 5   THEN 'Up to 5 employees' 
                WHEN EmpCount >= 5  AND EmpCount <= 15 THEN '5 to 15 employees'
                WHEN EmpCount >= 16 AND EmpCount <= 50 THEN '16 to 50 employees' 
                WHEN EmpCount >= 51 AND EmpCount <= 100 THEN '51 to 100 employees' 
                WHEN EmpCount >= 101 AND EmpCount <= 250 THEN '101 to 250 employees' 
                WHEN EmpCount >= 251 AND EmpCount <= 500 THEN '251 to 500 employees'
                WHEN EmpCount >= 501 THEN 'Above 500' 
            END AS No_of_Employers_having_Employees,  
            COUNT(*) AS EmployerCount 
        FROM (
            SELECT 
                COALESCE(A.Name, 'blanks') AS AccountName, 
                COUNT(DISTINCT V.Id) AS EmpCount 
            FROM SF_Visa_LK_LOAD V  
            LEFT JOIN SF_Account_LK_LOAD A ON V.Sponsoring_Company__c = A.Id 
            WHERE V.Visa_Issue_Date__c IS NOT NULL 
              AND V.Renewed_By_Visa__c IS NULL 
              AND V.Visa_Validity_Status__c = 'Issued' 
              AND V.Visa_Type__c <> 'Student' 
              AND (A.Is_Expo_Concession__c = false OR A.Is_Expo_Concession__c IS NULL)
              AND (A.EXPO_Participants_Registration__c = false OR A.EXPO_Participants_Registration__c IS NULL)
              AND (A.Is_Expo_Commercial__c = false OR A.Is_Expo_Commercial__c IS NULL) 
              AND A.Name <> 'International Trading (Test) 1' 
            GROUP BY A.Name
        ) Sub
        GROUP BY 
            CASE 
                WHEN EmpCount >= 1  AND EmpCount < 5   THEN 'Up to 5 employees' 
                WHEN EmpCount >= 5  AND EmpCount <= 15 THEN '5 to 15 employees'
                WHEN EmpCount >= 16 AND EmpCount <= 50 THEN '16 to 50 employees' 
                WHEN EmpCount >= 51 AND EmpCount <= 100 THEN '51 to 100 employees' 
                WHEN EmpCount >= 101 AND EmpCount <= 250 THEN '101 to 250 employees' 
                WHEN EmpCount >= 251 AND EmpCount <= 500 THEN '251 to 500 employees'
                WHEN EmpCount >= 501 THEN 'Above 500' 
            END;

29. Revenue
   - Primary Table: SF_Invoice_LK_LOAD1
   - SQL Query: SELECT SUM(R.Amount_Incl_VAT_KT__c) AS FullYearRevenue, 
                (SUM(R.Amount_Incl_VAT_KT__c) / 12) * MONTH(CURRENT_TIMESTAMP) AS YTDRevenue, 
                (SUM(R.Amount_Incl_VAT_KT__c) / 55000000) * 100 AS FullYearAchievedPercentage, 
                (SUM(R.Amount_Incl_VAT_KT__c) / ((55000000 / 12) * MONTH(CURRENT_TIMESTAMP))) * 100 AS YTDAchievedPercentage 
                FROM SF_Invoice_LK_LOAD1 I 
                LEFT JOIN SF_InvoiceLineItem_LK_LOAD ILI ON I.Id = ILI.Invoice__c 
                LEFT JOIN SF_Receipt_Template_LK_LOAD R ON ILI.Service__c = R.Id 
                WHERE I.Service_Provider__c = 'Free Zone' 
                AND YEAR(I.Payment_Date__c) = YEAR(CURRENT_TIMESTAMP);
   - Annual Target for Free Zone - (eg. 2025-> 55M)
